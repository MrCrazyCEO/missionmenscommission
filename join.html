Ways to Join

Whether you need support or want to support others, there's a place for you

District WhatsApp Groups

Join your local district WhatsApp group for immediate connection with men in your area. We have groups covering all 14 districts of Kerala for localized, relevant support.

Online Portal

Register on our online portal to share concerns, access resources, and receive systematic follow-up from our dedicated team members.

Training & Workshops

Participate in our regular training sessions, awareness programs, and knowledge-sharing workshops designed to empower and educate.

Volunteer

Support the mission by volunteering your time, skills, or expertise. Help us reach more men who need support and expand our impact.
