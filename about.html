About Mission Men's Commission

A compassionate initiative changing the narrative for men across Kerala

Our Story

Born from Recognition and Compassion

Mission Men's Commission is a compassionate, community-driven initiative founded by Shri. Rahul Easwar to address the silent struggles faced by men across Kerala. Born from the recognition that men often suffer in isolation due to societal expectations and stigma, this initiative provides a safe space where men can voice their concerns without fear of judgment.

We understand that men face unique challenges in today's complex social landscapeâ€”from baseless allegations and legal harassment to mental health crises that often go unnoticed until it's too late. Too many men have nowhere to turn when they're struggling, leading some to tragic endpoints. Mission Men's Commission exists to change this narrative.

At our core, we believe in the power of listening. When a man reaches out to usâ€”whether through our WhatsApp district groups or online portalâ€”he's met first with empathy and understanding. We don't judge; we listen. Then, based on his specific needs, we connect him with the right professionals who can help.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

  • To build a supportive and inclusive platform for men across Kerala
  • To ensure men's voices and concerns are heard and acknowledged
  • To promote mental health awareness and emotional well-being
  • To create a society where men can seek help without stigma
  • To empower men with knowledge, counseling, and legal awareness

Our Mission

  • To provide access to professional counselors and legal experts
  • To raise awareness about men's issues, rights, and challenges
  • To build district-level communities for local support and connection
  • To stand by men in their low moments through empathy and solidarity
  • To conduct training and knowledge-sharing sessions for men's growth
  • To create a safe, non-judgmental environment for open discussions

The Vision Behind the Commission

Rahul Easwar's journey into men's advocacy stems from a deep understanding of societal dynamics and a genuine concern for those who suffer in silence. His advocacy recognizes that while support systems exist for women and children, men often find themselves without institutional backing when facing false allegations, mental health crises, or legal harassment.

What sets his approach apart is the emphasis on inclusivity and understanding. Rather than creating opposition to existing support systems, the mission is about filling a gapâ€”ensuring that every citizen, regardless of gender, has somewhere to turn when life becomes overwhelming.
